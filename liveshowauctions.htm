2011 Pioneer Classic Auctions

Pioneer Classic will be opening certain silent auction pieces up for proxy bids. All of these items were generously donated by fellow hobbyists, which we appreciate greatly! Click Here to see the list of donators.

If you are interested in placing a bid on an item, please e-mail me at tara at taverstables dot com. Please include which item it is you would like to bid on and how much your high bid will be. Please remember that YOUR BID IS A CONTRACT. Do not bid if you are unsure or will not pay, should you win the auction. Winning bidder, if not present at the show, will pay reasonable shipping costs via USPS Priority Mail. Payment can be accepted via Paypal or money orders. I will consider checks from those with whom I have worked with before in a positive manner. I will keep the auctions album updated with initials of the high bidder and the current high bid.

The live show will be held on Saturday, August 6, 2011 where showers will have the opportunity to bid on the items. The starting bid for the public auctions will be the high bid placed by a proxy bidder. Showers will have the last chance to win an item, so if you really want a particular item, bid high!

Bidding via proxy will be accepted until midnight on Thursday, August 4.

If you have questions, please feel free to e-mail me and ask. I have all of the items in hand. Thank you so much for considering these auctions!

This year, all of the silent auction items are on a Webshots Album.
