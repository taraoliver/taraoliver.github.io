COOKIE CONTEST:

Pudding Cookies – entered by Ashley Etters

 

2 ¼ c. flour

1 t. baking soda

1 c. butter softened

¾ c. packed brown sugar

¼  c. granulated sugar

1 pkg (4-serving) instant pudding

1 t. vanilla

2 eggs

1 12 oz. package chocolate chips

1 c. nuts (optional)

 

Sometimes I use…

Vanilla pudding with chocolate chips

Chocolate pudding with vanilla chips

Chocolate pudding with peanut butter chips

(As a side note from Tara – why not do vanilla pudding with ALL three chips? Yummmm!!)

 

Mix flour with baking soda. Set aside.

Combine butter, the sugars, pudding mix, and vanilla in a large mixer bowl. Beat until smooth and creamy.

Beat in eggs.

Gradually add flour mixture then stir in chips and nuts. Batter will be stiff.

Drop from teaspoon onto baking sheets about 2 inches apart.

Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes.

Makes about 7 dozen cookies.

 

Rolo Cookies

 

2 1/2 c. flour

3/4 c. cocoa (I used special dark)

1 tsp.baking soda

1 c. sugar

1 c. packed brown sugar

1 c. softened butter

2 tsp. vanilla

2 eggs

bag of rolos

 

Beat sugars, butter, eggs and vanilla. Add flour, cocoa and soda. Shape 1 tablespoon of dough around each rolo.

 

Bake at 350 F for 5 to 7 minutes. Let set 2 minutes before removing from pan. Can additional toppings like melted white chocolate or sugar-cocoa mix.
